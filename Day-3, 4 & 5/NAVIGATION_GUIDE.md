## ğŸ¯ Quick Access Tips

### **To Sign Up:**
1. Click **Login icon** in header
2. Click **"Sign Up"** link at bottom
3. Or go directly to: `signup.html`

### **To View About/Contact/FAQ:**
1. Look at **header navigation**
2. Or scroll to **footer** and click Quick Links

### **To Access Admin:**
1. Go to `admin.html` directly
2. Or login with admin credentials and you'll be redirected

### **To View Cart:**
1. Click **cart icon** in header (top right)
2. Or go directly to: `cart.html`

### **To Checkout:**
1. Add items to cart
2. Click cart icon
3. Click **"Proceed to Checkout"**
